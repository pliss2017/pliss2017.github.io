Registration

The Summer School is open to anyone interested. Prerequisites are knowledge of programming languages implementation at the level provided by an undergraduate survey course.

To register please provide your full name, gender, country of origin, minority group (if applicable), along with the following:

  • A letter of recommendation from your advisor(s).
  • Current academic status.
  • Brief description of your future academic/industry plans/goals.

To the email address: plis-bertinoro@googlegroups.com

Materials should be received by April 1, 2017. Foreign (Non-EU) participants should begin immediately to obtain travel documents.

Housing

To keep costs low, students and speakers will be able to stay on campus.

Payment

Students are required to pay for their registration to the summer school and for their dinners. Students will stay in University Residential Centre of Bertinoro campus, with breakfast and lunch included.
